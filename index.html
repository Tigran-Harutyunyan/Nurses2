how it works
Simple steps on how staffers and nurses connect on demand

  • Staffer needs to staff a nurse for the job assignment.

  • Staffer creates job assegnment with detaials of location, type of nurse and level of care needed.

  • After job assignment has been created a notification will be shared with all nurses that are within proximity.

  • Nurses will receive instant push notification with new job assignment detail.

  • Staffer will receive the job acceptance available nurses and approve assignment to desired canditate.

  • Selected nurse will proceed with completion of assegnment.

  • After completion of the job assegnment, staffer will be able to rate and write a review on the assigned nurse.
