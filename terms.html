Background Checks

YourNurses.com offers the following types of background checks:

  1. Preliminary background checks
  2. Preferred and premier background checks (collectively "Background Checks")

These background checks must be ordered by nurses about themselves on their own initiative or to increase their employment chances by providers or in response to a provider considering them for employment. Premier background checks ordered by the provider are subject to the written authorization of the nurse who is the subject of the check. Other background checks require payment of a separate fee by the person initiating the check request.

The Fair Credit Reporting Act (FCRA) regulates each background check. The background reports resulting from these services are considered "consumer reports" by the FCRA. Consumer reports may contain information on your character, reputation, personal characteristics, mode of living, consumer credit, criminal history, workers compensation, driving, employment, military, civil and educational data and documents. YourNurses.com will receive a copy of each background check you order or authorize through the website. If you order a preliminary, preferred or preferred and background check about yourself, you will be emailed a copy if it does not come back clean, You are responsible for making sure the email addresses you provide to us are correct and acknowledge that it will receive sensitive information.

If you order any of the above background checks in response to a provider request and the check comes back clean or does not result in us suspending or terminating your account, we will forward the check to the provider with your consent. The results of any of these checks that result in suspension or termination of your account will not be shared with other users.

If you provide written authorization for a provider to order a premier background check, the check will be sent directly to the provider. In this situation, the provider will receive the background check regardless of its contents.

If you have ordered a background check through our website, we may indicate in your profile that you have completed the check. We will not share the results with any other website visitor or registered user without your written consent.

The conditions for using, reviewing and/or requesting background checks are as follows:

  1. Nurses can order or authorize background checks on themselves and share them with other registered members.
  2. YourNurses.com may review and use background checks nurses have ordered.
  3. YourNurses.com may order and use background checks on nurses.
  4. YourNurses.com may regularly verify your identity and the accuracy of your claims

When using background checks with YourNurses.com, you acknowledge that the background checks may not be completely accurate. Furthermore, YourNurses.com is not liable for any dispute that may arise from background checks.

Providers Under FCRA Special Responsibilities

Your responsibilities for using the information in the background check can be obtained at http://www.ftc.gov/os/statutes/fcrajump.shtm. FCRA governs the use of reports acquired from consumer reporting agencies.
